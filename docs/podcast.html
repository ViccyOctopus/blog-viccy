quantum theory for the ears: a podcast

i remember taking physics in highschool sophomore year. it was pretty difficult, mostly because i had a horrible teacher. i ended up getting a B in the class, my first and last B in highschool.

but, after watching a ted talk, i was inspired to learn more about the idea of quantum entanglement. spotaneously, i bought a book by the MIT Press Essential Knowledge Series on quantum entanglement and read it feverishly. now, here i am, inspired and with my own podcast.

new episodes come out on average every 2-3 weeks :)
